DAVIS Challenge on Video Object Segmentation 2017

Workshop in conjunction with CVPR 2017, Honolulu, Hawaii

The DAVIS Challenge on Video Object Segmentation 2017 will be held as a workshop in CVPR 2017, Honolulu, Hawaii. It will showcase and discuss the results of the DAVIS Test-Challenge phase.

News

The 2017 DAVIS Challenge finished with 22 submissions. Thanks all!
Test-dev submissions will remain open in the codalab site of the challenge.
